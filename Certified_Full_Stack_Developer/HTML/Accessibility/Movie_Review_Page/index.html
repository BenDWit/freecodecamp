johnny mnemonic

Johnny Mnemonic (1995) is a cyberpunk action-thriller set in a dystopian future where information is smuggled in human brains. Keanu Reeves stars as Johnny, a data courier with a cybernetic implant that stores sensitive data too large for his memory, putting his life at risk. As he races against time and powerful corporations to deliver the information, he uncovers a conspiracy that could save a world ravaged by technological overload. The film blends gritty sci-fi aesthetics with themes of digital dependency, AI, and corporate control.
